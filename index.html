Joy Ride

The car is where I ponder

To the window, my eyes wander

I curl my legs up on the chair

As we carry on to anywhere

As trees loose form in speed

I feel the need to pee

My bladder cannot wait

I envy a younger me

One who didn't suffer the need

To release the muscles from the tension
